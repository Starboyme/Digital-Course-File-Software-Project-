About

An interactive Web Application for managing the class materials, question papers, etc. The faculties are given their login credentials and they can upload the class materials which is to be shared to their students. The students also have their login credentials and they get access to the files uploaded by their mentor under the respective courses. We are still in learning phase, so we welcome your reviews and you can reach through mail. (filespro@gmail.com)
